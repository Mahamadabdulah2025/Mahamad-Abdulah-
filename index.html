01 — About

Who I Am

I’m Mahamad Abdulah, a Solar Design Engineer currently working with Anchor by Panasonic India in Bengaluru. Originally from Birgunj, Nepal, I hold a Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Cambridge Institute of Technology, Bangalore (CGPA: 7.5/10).

My core expertise lies in Solar PV system design — performing system sizing from load analysis, selecting modules and inverters, carrying out electrical calculations (string sizing, voltage drop, cable sizing), and reviewing SLDs and BOMs. I also provide design training to Graduate Engineer Trainees on rooftop and ground-mount PV workflows.

I am TÜV India certified and trained in ISO 9001:2015 internal auditing. Proficient in AutoCAD, PVsyst, and SolarLabs (Arka 360), and currently expanding my skills in SketchUp 3D for advanced visualisation of solar installations.

“Passionate about clean energy — I stay updated with emerging solar technologies, MNRE guidelines, and industry best practices to drive innovation in Solar EPC projects.”
